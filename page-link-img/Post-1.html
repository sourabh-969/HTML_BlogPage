  • Cartoon:  A broader term encompassing all types of animation, including anime, regardless of origin or style.

Here are some of the most popular genres in anime:

  • Shonen:  Aimed at a young male audience, featuring action-packed stories with heroic protagonists, such as "Naruto","Bleach", and "My Hero Academia".

  • Shojo:  Targeted towards young female audiences, often focusing on romance, drama, and personal relationships, like "Sailor Moon", "Fruits Basket", and "Kimi ni Todoke".

  • Seinen:  Intended for mature male audiences, with more complex, adult-oriented themes and content, such as "Cowboy Bebop", "Ghost in the Shell", and "Vinland Saga".

  • Slice of Life:  Depicting the everyday lives of characters, often with a focus on mundane activities and personal growth,like "Nichijou", "K-On!", and "Komi Can't Communicate".

  • Fantasy/Adventure:  Featuring fantastical worlds, magical elements, and thrilling quests, such as "Fullmetal Alchemist","Re:Zero", and "Made in Abyss".
  • Mecha:  Centered around giant, humanoid mechanical robots, often with intricate designs and complex narratives, like"Neon Genesis Evangelion", "Code Geass", and "Gundam".

  • Psychological/Thriller:  Exploring the depths of the human psyche and tackling intense, thought-provoking themes, such as"Death Note", "Steins;Gate", and "Parasyte".
